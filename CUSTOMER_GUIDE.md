# Customer User Guide

Welcome to the Organic Vegetable Order Management System! This guide will help you place orders, track deliveries, and manage your account.

## Getting Started

### 1. Login

1. Visit the website and click "Login"
2. Enter your phone number (with country code, e.g., +27123456789) or email address
3. Click "Send Verification Code"
4. Check your WhatsApp or email for a 6-digit code
5. Enter the code on the verification page
6. You're now logged in!

**Note**: Verification codes expire after 10 minutes. If your code expires, request a new one.

## Placing an Order

### 1. Add Items to Cart

1. Browse the product catalog
2. Click "Add to Cart" on any product
3. The cart icon in the header shows your item count
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### 2. Review Your Cart

1. Click the cart icon in the header
2. Review your selected items
3. Adjust quantities using the +/- buttons
4. Remove items by clicking the trash icon
5. See your order total at the bottom

### 3. Checkout

1. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
2. Select your delivery method:
   - **Delivery**: Choose Monday, Wednesday, or Friday
   - **Collection**: Pick up your order yourself
3. Confirm or update your delivery address
4. Add any special instructions (e.g., "Leave at gate")
5. Review your order summary
6. Click "Place Order"

**Important**: Orders must be placed by **Friday** for the following week's deliveries.

### 4. Order Confirmation

After placing your order:
- You'll see a confirmation message
- You'll receive a WhatsApp/email notification with order details
- Your cart will be cleared
- You can view the order in your order history

## Managing Your Account

### View Order History

1. Click your name in the header
2. Select "My Orders"
3. See all your past orders with:
   - Order date
   - Delivery date
   - Order status (Pending, Confirmed, Delivered)
   - Total amount
4. Click any order to see full details

### Check Credit Balance

Your credit balance is displayed prominently on your profile page. Credits are applied automatically to future invoices.

**You receive credits for**:
- Overpayments on invoices
- Short deliveries (when we can't fulfill part of your order)

### Update Profile

1. Click your name in the header
2. Select "Profile"
3. Update your:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Email address
   - Delivery address
4. Click "Save Changes"

## Understanding Invoices

### When You Get an Invoice

After your order is delivered, the admin will generate an invoice. You'll receive it via:
- Email (PDF attachment)
- WhatsApp (link to download)

### Invoice Details

Your invoice shows:
- Itemized list of products with quantities and prices
- Subtotal
- Credits applied (if any)
- Final total due

### Payment Methods

You can pay using:
- **Cash**: Pay on delivery or at collection
- **Yoco**: Card payment (contact admin)
- **EFT**: Bank transfer (details on invoice)

**Note**: The admin records payments in the system. Your invoice status will update once payment is recorded.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Login?

- Verify your phone number includes country code (+27...)
- Check WhatsApp/email for the verification code
- Request a new code if it expired
- Contact admin if issues persist

### Didn't Receive Verification Code?

- Check spam/junk folder for emails
- Verify your phone number is correct
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact admin for assistance

### Order Not Showing?

- Refresh the page
- Check your order history
- Verify you completed the checkout process
- Contact admin if order is missing

### Wrong Delivery Address?

- Update your profile before placing orders
- Add delivery notes in special instructions
- Contact admin immediately if you need to change an existing order

### Payment Questions?

- Check your invoice for payment details
- Contact admin to confirm payment received
- Ask about payment method options
